Welcome to the Buddy Program

Why should you become a buddy?

  • Cultural Exchange: Engage in enriching experiences by learning about and sharing your own culture.
  • Language Practice: Improve and practice language skills through regular, real-world conversation.
  • Networking: Expand your social and professional circles with connections across borders.
  • Personal Growth: Enhance social skills and cultural awareness, fostering empathy and global perspective.
  • Friendship: Forge lasting friendships and a support network that goes beyond the program.

Benefits for Exchange Students

  • Local Insights: Gain valuable insights into local customs and practices from a home student's perspective.
  • Academic Support: Receive guidance and support on academic matters from someone familiar with the institution.
  • Integration: A buddy can help ease the transition into a new environment, making integration smoother and faster.
  • Social Opportunities: Access to a wider social network and invitations to local events that might otherwise be missed.
  • Practical Help: Get practical help with day-to-day challenges such as navigating the city, shopping, or understanding local services.
